Services

Five core areas. Each is built around what actually fixes cars and protects owners: real diagnostics, calibration with mechanical context, restoration judged by value, builds that don’t waste budget.

Diagnostics

What it includes: Electrical fault tracing, drivability diagnosis, module-level and CAN-based analysis. We establish root cause before recommending repair or calibration.

Who it’s for: Owners with intermittent or complex faults, check-engine or drivability issues, or cars that have been modified and need a clear baseline.

Why a specialist: Mercedes/AMG systems need platform-specific tooling and procedures. We don’t guess—we trace.

Calibration

What it includes: ECU/TCU strategy with mechanical context. Road-tested, data-informed. Power delivery, drivability, and reliability as one package.

Who it’s for: Owners who want more from the platform without sacrificing daily usability or creating new problems.

Why a specialist: Calibration only works when it’s aligned with hardware, cooling, and how the car is actually used. We do that under one roof.

Restoration

What it includes: Mechanical and cosmetic restoration to preservation-grade fit and finish. Judged by long-term value and presentation that holds up.

Who it’s for: Collector-era and high-value Mercedes owners who want work that protects or increases value, not quick cover-ups.

Why a specialist: Concours-informed standards and platform-specific judgment. We don’t do “good enough”—we do right.

Performance integration

What it includes: Powertrain, cooling, and drivability systems planned and executed as one integrated package. No orphan mods, no conflicting tune and hardware.

Who it’s for: Owners building for more power or track use who want a coherent result, not a list of parts.

Why a specialist: Staging and integration prevent wasted spend and rework. We sequence the work so it works.

Specialty build planning

What it includes: Full build strategy, systems sequencing, milestone-based scope, and execution that avoids surprises and protects budget.

Who it’s for: Owners with ambitious builds who want one clear roadmap and a shop that can execute it.

Why a specialist: We’ve done the diagnostics and calibration side for years. Build planning is an extension of that—we know what order things have to happen in.
