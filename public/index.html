What we do

Services

Five core areas. Each is Mercedes/AMG-focused and tied to real outcomes: clearer faults, better drivability, restoration that protects value, builds that don’t waste budget.

Diagnostics

Electrical, drivability, and module-level fault tracing. We establish the real problem before recommending work. No guesswork, no unnecessary parts.

Calibration

ECU/TCU strategy with mechanical context. Road-tested, data-informed. Clean power delivery and drivability, not just peak numbers.

Restoration

Mechanical and cosmetic restoration to preservation-grade fit and finish. Judged by long-term value and concours-informed presentation.

Specialty builds

Staged build planning, systems integration, and execution that avoids wasted budget and rework. One coherent package, not piecemeal mods.

FAQ

Do you only work on Mercedes and AMG?

Yes. Process, tooling, and calibration are Mercedes/AMG-focused. We do not take other makes.

Can I start with diagnostics only?

Yes. Diagnostics-first is the recommended path before locking in performance or restoration scope.

Do you do staged projects?

Yes. Projects can be phased by budget, parts, and timeline.

Why a specialist over a dealer or general shop?

Specialists deliver platform-specific judgment, deeper diagnostics, and a finished result that matches what serious owners expect—not generic service.

Do you work on already-modified cars?

Yes. First step is establishing what’s on the car, what works, and what’s causing compromise.

Will you advise against the wrong next step?

Yes. Our job includes protecting your budget and the platform, not selling line items.
